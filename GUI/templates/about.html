About Skin Disease Classification System

The Skin Disease Classification System is designed to assist dermatologists in diagnosing skin diseases by using artificial intelligence to analyze images of skin conditions. This system uses deep learning algorithms to provide an initial assessment of skin images, which can help streamline the diagnostic process and provide quick feedback to patients.

Our system is built on state-of-the-art convolutional neural networks that have been trained on thousands of images from the HAM10000 dataset. These models are capable of recognizing multiple types of skin diseases with high accuracy, providing a valuable second opinion to healthcare professionals.

Disclaimer: This tool is intended to be used as a supplementary aid in diagnosis and is not intended to replace professional medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider with any questions regarding a medical condition.
